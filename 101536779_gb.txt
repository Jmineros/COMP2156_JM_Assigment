George Brown College (GBC) is a public college of applied arts and technology in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. 
Founded in 1967, GBC offers a variety of diploma, certificate, degree, and postgraduate programs in 
business, technology, health sciences, hospitality, and the arts.

The college has three main campuses: 
1. **Casa Loma Campus** – Focuses on technology and construction programs.
2. **St. James Campus** – Home to business, hospitality, and creative arts programs.
3. **Waterfront Campus** – Specializes in health sciences and nursing.

George Brown College emphasizes experiential learning, with strong industry connections, 
work-integrated learning opportunities, and state-of-the-art facilities. 

For more details, visit: [https://www.georgebrown.ca](https://www.georgebrown.ca)
