George Brown College (GBC) is a leader in hands-on education located in Toronto, Ontario.
Established in 1967, GBC has grown to become one of Canada's top colleges.
The college operates three main campuses: St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront.
GBC offers over 160 full-time programs in areas like technology, business, and health sciences.
The college emphasizes experiential learning through internships, co-ops, and labs.
The Waterfront campus features advanced labs for health sciences programs.
