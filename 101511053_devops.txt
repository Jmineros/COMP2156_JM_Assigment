Course Objectives:

DevOps Fundamentals: Understand the core concepts and cultural aspects of DevOps, focusing on collaboration between development and operations teams.

Infrastructure as Code (IaC): Gain proficiency in using tools that allow infrastructure management through code, ensuring consistency and scalability.

Monitoring and Logging: Develop skills to monitor applications and infrastructure, enabling proactive issue detection and resolution.
